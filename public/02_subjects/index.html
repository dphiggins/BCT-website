Our Enrichment Courses
We are now offering 1 enrichment course
Course: Introduction to Computer Science

When: Course will begin on August 8th, 2020, and meet twice a week until September 12th, 2020. To get a better understanding of the schedule and start times of classes you can read our syllabus.


We hope to provide a fun and engaging place for kids to interact with one another while learning a useful skill, and hopefully inspire them to further pursue computer science. In this class-like environment, kids will work together to solve problems and further their quantitative reasoning skills. Additionally, this course offers a unique experience for kids to interact with one another, especially during these current times, while still being able to learn and receive adequate help from an instructor. No prior experience is required, and beginners are encouraged to take the course.
