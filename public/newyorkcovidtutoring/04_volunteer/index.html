Volunteer

If you have an interest in volunteering for us, please submit the google form below. This will let us know what academic areas you can tutor. We will soon contact you and set up a time for an interview. See below for some rough guidelines for what we want in a tutor:

  • Flexible hours to tutor (at least 4 days with 3 hours though you won’t have to be tutoring this often if you don’t want to).

  • 2 or more subjects that you feel comfortable tutoring (we will make sure that you are qualified to teach your subjects).

  • Good communication (to the tutee, to us, etc.).
